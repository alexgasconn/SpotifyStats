How to get your full Spotify history (ZIP)

  1. Open your Spotify account privacy page: Privacy settings.
  2. Go to Download your data.
  3. Request Extended streaming history (not only the recent account data).
  4. Wait for Spotify's email and download the ZIP file.
  5. Upload that ZIP here for full-year and multi-year analysis.
